Storyboard

Visual storytelling that sets the foundation for great animation

Storyboarding & Visual Narrative

Storyboards are the blueprint of animation. As a Lead Story Artist, I translate scripts into visual sequences that guide every aspect of production. My boards don't just show what happens—they capture the emotion, pacing, and visual language that makes stories come alive.

What I Deliver

  • Thumbnail Sequences: Quick visual exploration of scenes and beats
  • Detailed Storyboards: Comprehensive panels with camera angles, staging, and action
  • Animatics: Timed storyboard sequences with sound and pacing
  • Beat Boards: Key emotional and narrative moments visualized
  • Revision & Polish: Refining boards based on director feedback

My Approach

I begin by understanding the script's emotional core and narrative structure. Then I explore visual approaches that enhance the story—finding the right camera angles, compositions, and pacing. Every panel serves multiple purposes: advancing the plot, revealing character, and creating visual interest.

Collaboration

Storyboarding is inherently collaborative. I work closely with directors, writers, and other department heads to ensure the boards serve the story while being practical for production. My experience allows me to anticipate animation needs and create boards that guide rather than constrain.

Experience

I've created storyboards for feature films, television series, and commercial projects. My work has been the foundation for productions that have won awards and touched millions of viewers worldwide.
