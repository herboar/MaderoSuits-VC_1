About

Hello, I'm Marek. Welcome to Animarek Studios.

For over a decade, I've been crafting stories through the art of animation. As an industry veteran, I've worn many hats—from Animation Director and Lead Story Artist to Character Designer and Art Director—on productions that have touched audiences worldwide.

Animarek Studios is where experience meets experimentation. We don't just create animation; we craft experiences that resonate, inspire, and endure. Every frame tells a story. Every character breathes life into imagination.

Whether it's a sweeping feature film, an intimate short, or a groundbreaking series, we believe in the power of animation to transform ideas into unforgettable journeys.

Let's bring your vision to life.
