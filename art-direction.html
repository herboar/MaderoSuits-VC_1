Art Direction

Establishing the visual language and aesthetic of your project

Art Direction & Visual Development

Art direction is about creating a cohesive visual world that serves the story. As an Art Director, I establish the aesthetic language, color palettes, lighting, and overall visual style that defines a project's look and feel.

What I Create

  • Visual Style Guides: Comprehensive documents defining the project's aesthetic
  • Color Scripts: Color palettes that enhance mood and narrative progression
  • Environment Design: Establishing the look and feel of worlds and spaces
  • Lighting Keys: Defining how light shapes mood and atmosphere
  • Material Studies: Textures, surfaces, and material properties
  • Mood Boards: Visual references and inspiration collections

My Process

I begin by deeply understanding the story's themes, tone, and emotional journey. Then I explore visual approaches that enhance these elements. Through extensive research, experimentation, and collaboration, I develop a visual language that is both distinctive and functional for production.

Collaboration

Art direction requires close collaboration with directors, writers, and all visual departments. I ensure that every visual element—from character design to environments to lighting—works together to create a cohesive, compelling world that serves the story.

Experience

I've served as Art Director on multiple productions, establishing visual styles that have become defining elements of those projects. My work spans feature films, series, and innovative short-form content.
